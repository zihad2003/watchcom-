A CENTURY OF PRECISION

1924
Born in Geneva
Founded in the mountain ateliers of Switzerland, AETERNA produced its first pocket movement with 19 jewels.
1948
The First Diver
AETERNA introduced its iconic 100m water-resistant diver, redefining adventure horology.
1972
The Mechanical Revolution
An in-house movement beating at 28,800 vph became the industry benchmark.
2001
The Titan Era
Grade-5 titanium cases, pioneering lightweight luxury for a new generation.
